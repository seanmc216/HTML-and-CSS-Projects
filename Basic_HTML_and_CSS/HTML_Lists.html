Sauteéd Baby Octopus

  1. Prepare the octopus
    • Defrost
    • Clear out the head
    • Rinse
    • Sauteé under boiling water for 1-2 minutes
    • Heat in a pan for 8 minutes
  2. Prepare the vegetables
    • Dice the tomato
    • Cook the diced potato
    • Slice the olives
  3. Final preparations
    • Add salt, pepper and garlic whilst cooking the octopus
    • Add the vegetables to the pan and cook together for a final few minutes

    Important Considerations

  • Buy pre-prepared octopus if available so that you only have to remove the head.
  • After preparation, cover the octopus with boiling water for 1-2 minutes but no longer or they will shrink.
  • Make sure the octopus are cooked evenly once in the pan.
